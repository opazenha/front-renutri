# App Functionalities
<appFunctionalities>
  <generalAppFunctionalities>
    <functionality>Secure multi-patient platform access for nutritionists.</functionality>
    <functionality>User authentication and authorization (nutritionist login).</functionality>
    <functionality>Modular design mirroring assessment categories.</functionality>
    <functionality>Comprehensive data validation for all user inputs.</functionality>
    <functionality>Clean, intuitive, and responsive user interface.</functionality>
    <functionality>Generate printable summaries and reports for individual patients.</functionality>
    <functionality>Robust search and filtering capabilities for patients and their assessment data.</functionality>
    <functionality>Consistent use and display of specified units (Kg, cm, Kcal, mg/dl, etc.).</functionality>
    <functionality>Timestamping of all assessments and records.</functionality>
    <functionality>Ensure patient data is stored securely with appropriate access controls.</functionality>
    <functionality>Link related data across modules (e.g., patient ID linking all assessments to a patient).</functionality>
  </generalAppFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Cadastro (Patient Registration)">
    <functionality>Create new patient demographic records.</functionality>
    <functionality>Read/View existing patient records.</functionality>
    <functionality>Update existing patient records.</functionality>
    <functionality>Delete patient records.</functionality>
    <functionality>Automatically generate a unique Patient ID upon creation.</functionality>
    <functionality>Automatically calculate patient's Age based on Birth Date and current date.</functionality>
    <functionality>Automatically populate Registration Date with the current date or allow selection.</functionality>
    <functionality>Search for patients by various criteria (e.g., name, ID).</functionality>
    <functionality>List all registered patients.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Av. Clínica (Clinical Assessment)">
    <functionality>Record detailed clinical anamnesis for a selected patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow creation of multiple clinical assessments per patient, distinguished by date.</functionality>
    <functionality>Automatically populate patient's personal data (Name, Birth Date, Age, Sex, Schooling) from the Cadastro module.</functionality>
    <functionality>Utilize dropdown menus with predefined options for standardized data entry (e.g., birth term, bowel function, yes/no questions).</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow free-text input for narrative sections (e.g., main complaint, medical history).</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, edit, and delete clinical assessment records.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Av. Alimentar (Food Assessment)">
    <functionality>Record patient's dietary habits, history with nutritional counseling, allergies, and intolerances.</functionality>
    <functionality>Link food assessments to a specific patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow creation of multiple, timestamped food assessments per patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Use dropdowns for predefined options (e.g., appetite, previous counseling professional).</functionality>
    <functionality>Manage detailed meal information (e.g., typical meals like Desjejum, Colação, Almoço, etc., and their composition).</functionality>
    <functionality>Record 24-hour Food Recall data: input for each meal including time, food item, quantity/portion size, preparation method, and observations.</functionality>
    <functionality>Record Food Frequency Questionnaire (FFQ) data: input for food items/groups, frequency of consumption, and usual portion size.</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, edit, and delete food assessment records.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Av. Comportamental (Behavioral Assessment)">
    <functionality>Record patient's smoking habits (status, type, frequency, quantity, history).</functionality>
    <functionality>Record patient's alcohol consumption habits.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow dynamic addition of consumed alcoholic beverage types, with fields for frequency, quantity per occasion, unit of measure, and alcohol content.</functionality>
    <functionality>Optionally, calculate total alcohol intake based on recorded data.</functionality>
    <functionality>Record details of physical activity (type(s), frequency, duration per session, intensity).</functionality>
    <functionality>Record patient's stress level and perceived quality of life.</functionality>
    <functionality>Link behavioral assessments to a specific patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow creation of multiple, timestamped behavioral assessments per patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, edit, and delete behavioral assessment records.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Evolução (Patient Evolution)">
    <functionality>Track and display patient progress over time.</functionality>
    <functionality>Aggregate and display a chronological log or dashboard of key metrics (e.g., weight, BMI, specific lab results, changes in food habits) collected from other modules.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow nutritionists to add timestamped narrative notes about patient progress.</functionality>
    <functionality>Visualize patient progress using charts (e.g., weight over time, body fat % over time, lab value trends).</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Av. Bioquímica (Biochemical Assessment)">
    <functionality>Record and track results of biochemical lab exams for a selected patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow entry of multiple lab exams per assessment date.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow multiple entries for each exam type over time to track changes.</functionality>
    <functionality>Use a predefined list of common exam names and allow adding custom exams.</functionality>
    <functionality>Record exam value, unit, collection date, and reference range.</functionality>
    <functionality>Record specific conditions relevant for interpreting exam results (e.g., Ferritina - Menarca, FSH - Fase folicular).</functionality>
    <functionality>Optionally, allow input of standard reference ranges for exams and automatically flag out-of-range values.</functionality>
    <functionality>Display biochemical data in tabular formats for comparison over time.</functionality>
    <functionality>Display biochemical data in graphical formats to visualize trends.</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, edit, and delete biochemical assessment records.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Av. Antropométrica (Anthropometric Assessment)">
    <functionality>Record and track patient's body measurements for a selected patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow creation of multiple anthropometric assessments per patient, distinguished by assessment date.</functionality>
    <functionality>Automatically populate patient's personal data (Name, Sex) from the Cadastro module.</functionality>
    <functionality>Record current weight, usual weight, desired weight, height, circumferences (various sites), skinfolds (various sites), and bone diameters.</functionality>
    <functionality>Calculate Body Mass Index (BMI) from current weight and height.</functionality>
    <functionality>Calculate or allow selection of Body Frame (Compleição ou ossatura) based on measurements (e.g., wrist circumference/height ratio).</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, edit, and delete anthropometric assessment records.</functionality>
    <functionality>Track changes in anthropometric data over time through multiple assessments.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) Calculation">
    <functionality>Calculate Gross Energy Expenditure (GET) based on Metabolic Equivalent of Task (MET) for a selected patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow creation of multiple MET calculations per patient, distinguished by date.</functionality>
    <functionality>Input Resting Energy Expenditure (GER) directly or calculate GER using standard formulas (e.g., Harris-Benedict, Mifflin-St Jeor) requiring inputs like weight, height, age, and sex from other modules.</functionality>
    <functionality>Input sleep duration (hours).</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow dynamic addition of physical activities, selecting from a predefined list with associated METS values or adding custom activities and METS values.</functionality>
    <functionality>Input duration for each physical activity.</functionality>
    <functionality>Record work/occupational activities, including time spent and METS value or occupational activity factor.</functionality>
    <functionality>Calculate Energy Expenditure during sleep (GE com sono).</functionality>
    <functionality>Calculate Energy Expenditure for each recorded activity.</functionality>
    <functionality>Calculate Total Daily Energy Expenditure (GET).</functionality>
    <functionality>Feed calculated GET results into the Macronutrients and Calories module for dietary planning.</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, edit, and delete MET calculation records.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Macronutrientes e Calorias (Macronutrients and Calories)">
    <functionality>Define and calculate dietary prescription protocols for macronutrients and calories for a selected patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Import or link to Total Energy Expenditure (GET) calculated in the MET module or from other estimation methods.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow selection of prescription protocols based on objective (e.g., Gain, Loss, Maintenance).</functionality>
    <functionality>Adjust protocols based on activity level and specific goals/conditions (e.g., Endurance, Hipertrofia, Gestante).</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow input of desired macronutrient distribution as percentages of total energy value or as grams per kg of body weight.</functionality>
    <functionality>Calculate total target daily calories based on GET and selected caloric objective (e.g., surplus for gain, deficit for loss).</functionality>
    <functionality>Calculate target grams for each macronutrient (Protein, Carbohydrates, Fat) based on the desired distribution and total target calories.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow creation and saving of multiple dietary plans/prescriptions per patient, distinguished by date or plan name.</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, edit, and delete dietary plan records.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>

  <moduleFunctionalities name="Micronutrientes (Micronutrients)">
    <functionality>Manage information and recommendations for minerals and electrolytes for a selected patient.</functionality>
    <functionality>Display Recommended Daily Allowances (RDAs) or Adequate Intakes (AIs) for various micronutrients.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow RDAs/AIs to be dynamic, adjusting based on patient's age, sex, and specific conditions (e.g., pregnancy, lactation) drawing from Cadastro and other relevant modules.</functionality>
    <functionality>Allow nutritionists to set specific micronutrient goals or targets for a patient, overriding or supplementing DRIs.</functionality>
    <functionality>Record details of prescribed micronutrient supplementation (e.g., dose, frequency, duration).</functionality>
    <functionality>Save, view, and edit micronutrient targets and prescribed supplementation records for a patient.</functionality>
    <functionality description="Future Enhancement">Integrate with a food logging system to compare actual micronutrient intake against recommendations and identify potential deficiencies or excesses.</functionality>
  </moduleFunctionalities>
</appFunctionalities>
